Title of the study: Interest in an inhaler recycling project in Edmonton, Alberta

Invitation to participate: You are invited to participate in this research study because you are picking up an inhaler prescription.

Purpose of the Study: Currently, there is no inhaler recycling program in Alberta.Â From this research we wish to determine the uptake of an inhaler recycling program.

Participation: If you wish to participate in this study, please complete the survey. The survey should take you approximately 5 minutes to complete. You do not have to answer any questions that you do not want to answer. Once you have completed the survey, if you have used the iPad please return it to the pharmacist.

Benefits: There may not be any direct personal benefits.

Risks: None.

Confidentiality and Anonymity: The information that you will share will remain strictly confidential and will be used solely for the purposes of this research. The only people who will have access to the research data are the research investigators. Results will be published in pooled (aggregate) format. Anonymity is guaranteed since you are not being asked to provide your name or any personal information.

Data Storage: Responses to the survey will be stored on Google Workspace on the University of Alberta server for a minimum of 5 years

Voluntary Participation: You are under no obligation to participate. Given the anonymous nature of the survey, once you have submitted your responses it will no longer be possible to withdraw them from the study.

Information about the Study Results: Research findings may be presented at medical conferences and published in a medical journal.

Contact Information: If you have any questions or require more information about the study itself, you may contact the researcher at the numbers mentioned herein.
